Hi. I'm Ruqi Zhang.

I am a 5th-year Ph.D. student in Statistics at Cornell University, advised by Chris De Sa. Before joining Cornell, I recieved a B.S. in Mathematics from Renmin University of China.

In Summer 2019, I interned at Microsoft Research Cambridge, working with Cheng Zhang, Yingzhen Li and Sam Devlin. In Summer 2020, I worked with Nicolo Fusi and Rishit Sheth at Microsoft Research New England.

I'm on the job market, looking for research positions in academia/industry.

Research Interests

I am interested in building scalable, reliable and efficient probabilistic models for machine learning and data science. Currently, I focus on developing fast and robust inference methods with theoretical guarantees and their applications with modern model architectures, such as deep neural networks, on real-world big data.
